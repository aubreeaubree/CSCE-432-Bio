About Me

Hello, my name is Aubree Rodriguez. I am a Computing major with a Cybersecurity minor at Texas A&M University. I enjoy front-end development and Geographic Information Systems (GIS). I believe that accessibility is an extremely important aspect of computing because the benefits of technology should be available to everyone. Throughout this course, I hope to learn about how we can design applications and websites to be easily usable by people of all different levels of ability and technological competency. In particular, I would love to explore ways of making technology more accesible to the elderly so that the gap between generations can be closed and the elderly can feel more connected to their friends, family, and the world at large!
